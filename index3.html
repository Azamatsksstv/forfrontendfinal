Bukpa

"Bukpa" is a traditional meat dish for the elderly, prepared at a wedding. The peculiarity of the dish is stewed meat. As a result of this technological method, the attractiveness of the product and its biological value increases. Mashed potatoes with pumpkin and demiglas sauce is a great addition to stews. If there are readers who have never tasted "Bukpa" - we recommend to book a table as soon as possible

Mi pilaf

Dear guests, let's continue the section of acquaintance with the cuisine of our restaurant. Today's dish is "Mi pilaf". "Mi pilaf" is lamb cooked on a bruschetta made of hand worms.
