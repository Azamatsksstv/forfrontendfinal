About us

“Oyu” means ornament. "Ornament in ancient times had a ritual meaning. For example, the Kazakhs had bronze and silver talismans with solar circles symbols of the sun, moon, sky, which they worshipped. The ornamented talisman was a sign of magic, a symbol of happiness and prosperity, the environment of religious beliefs. Almost all ornamental patterns were "read" at one time in a very definite way. The purpose of our restaurant with the name "Oyu" - to show the age-old traditions of cooking, eating, in general hospitality, and represent the unique items of historical and cultural significance, products of everyday life of Kazakhstan.

Unique Interior

The southern region of Kazakhstan has been famous for wood carving, pottery and carpet weaving, zergery and ustalyk, basketry and felting, sewing and embroidery from ancient times. We draw the attention of our visitors to the handicrafts that were once widely known outside the country - glassblowing and leatherwork, individual items and details from which, today, decorate the halls of the restaurant.
